Bio

I am a passionate researcher focused on the intersection of speech processing, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning. Currently pursuing a B.S. in Electrical Engineering at National Taiwan University, I have gained extensive experience in research at several top labs, including NTU Speech Processing and Machine Learning Lab, NTU Machine Intelligence & Understanding Lab, NTU Robot Learning Lab, Tsinghua Embodied AI Lab, IIIS. My recent work involves leveraging Meta In-Context Learning for Multilingual ASR and Diffusion Model-Augmented Behavioral Cloning. I have contributed to multiple prestigious publications and conferences, such as INTERSPEECH and ICML, and received honors such as the NTUEE Ma Tso-Ping Academician Future Innovation Leaders Scholarship and the NTUEE Undergraduate Innovation Award.
